Why Parents & Students Trust BrightPath NEET Academy

Founded in 2016, BrightPath NEET Academy was established with a revolutionary vision: medical entrance preparation should never be a factory-line process. Located in the heart of Malviya Nagar, Jaipur, we've become Rajasthan's most trusted NEET coaching destination.

Our unique methodology combines rigorous academic schedules with mental well-being programs and holistic student development, ensuring our aspirants stay motivated throughout their two-year journey or dropper year.
